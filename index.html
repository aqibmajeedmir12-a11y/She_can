Who We Are

Bridging the Opportunity Gap

She Can Foundation is a registered non-profit based in Kolkata, West Bengal. We assist female students and underprivileged youth in gaining digital literacy, professional vocational training, and career counseling to build sustainable livelihoods.

Through structured field campaigns, we foster self-reliance, community leadership, and equitable access — ensuring every aspiring woman can enter the professional world with confidence.

What We Do

Core Programs

Targeted initiatives addressing education, employment, and empowerment for women and youth.

Education Support

Direct scholarships, textbooks, and after-school tutoring for secondary-level female students in underfunded districts.

Women Empowerment

Structured workshops on gender equity, self-defense, reproductive health, and local leadership advocacy.

Skill Development

Hands-on bootcamps covering basic programming, digital tools, professional communication, and office IT.

Community Circles

Safe peer-learning spaces where adolescent girls network, collaborate, and access shared educational resources.

Career Mentorship

Connecting university students with working professionals in technology, management, and arts for guided career planning.

Mental Health Advocacy

Counseling access, emotional resilience workshops, and stress management programs for students and volunteers.

Our Journey

Milestones

From a local initiative to a state-recognized foundation serving hundreds of women.

2023

Foundation Launch

Registered in Kolkata. Launched initial tutorial drives reaching 50 girls in nearby communities.

2024

Digital Skill Labs

Opened our first computing facility. Enabled IT certifications for 200+ women, with 40 securing tech internships.

2025

Campus Partnerships

Partnered with 10 state colleges for mentorship structures. Volunteer network crossed 300 active members.

2026

National Outreach

Extending digital learning access to remote districts. Targeting 5,000+ students with specialized resources.
